# Use Case 4P Benefit 4I Risk Net Score ↓ Priority

Valoir ROI Scoring Methodology

4P — Benefit Dimensions (1–5)

People — % of employees or customers impacted
Process — complexity and volume of workflows automated
Price — cost of the activity or alternative being replaced
Potential — transformational impact beyond efficiency

4I — Risk Dimensions (1–5)

Intelligence — reasoning complexity and LLM call depth
Integration — number and complexity of data sources / APIs
Interaction — criticality of UX quality and fine-tuning needed
Importance — negative impact if the agent gives a wrong answer
4P Benefit avg = (People + Process + Price + Potential) ÷ 4
4I Risk avg = (Intelligence + Integration + Interaction + Importance) ÷ 4
Net Score = 4P Benefit avg − 4I Risk avg (range: −4.0 to +4.0)
